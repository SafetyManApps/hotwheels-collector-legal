Privacy Policy for Hot Wheels Collector

Effective Date: August 24, 2025

Quick Summary: We protect your privacy by storing your car collection locally on your device. We don't collect personal information, don't sell your data, and give you full control over your collection information.

Introduction

Hot Wheels Collector ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application Hot Wheels Collector (the "App").

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Car Collection Data: Car details you enter (name, manufacturer, series, year, color, condition, notes, estimated values, purchase information)
  • Photos: Images you take or select for your car collection
  • User Preferences: App settings, language preferences, theme choices
  • Purchase Information: In-app purchase records for Premium and Pro upgrades

Automatically Collected Information

  • Device Information: Device model, operating system version, unique device identifiers
  • App Usage Data: Features used, time spent in app, crash reports
  • Performance Data: App performance metrics for optimization purposes

Information We Do NOT Collect

  • Personal Identity: We do not collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, or other personal identifiers
  • Location Data: We do not access or store your location information
  • Contacts: We do not access your device contacts
  • External Storage: We only access photos you explicitly select for your collection

How We Use Your Information

Primary Uses

  • Core Functionality: Store and manage your Hot Wheels collection data
  • Feature Enhancement: Provide Premium and Pro features you've purchased
  • Data Backup: Enable cloud backup and restoration of your collection (Pro users)
  • App Improvement: Analyze usage patterns to improve app performance and features

We Do NOT

  • Sell Your Data: We never sell your personal information to third parties
  • Share Collection Data: Your car collection remains private to you
  • Use for Marketing: We do not use your data for advertising or marketing
  • Track Across Apps: We do not track your activity in other applications

Data Storage and Security

Local Storage

  • Device Storage: Your collection data is primarily stored locally on your device
  • Encrypted Database: Local data is stored in an encrypted SQLite database
  • Secure Photos: Images are stored securely in your device's private app directory

Cloud Storage (Pro Users Only)

  • Google Drive Integration: Pro users can optionally backup to their personal Google Drive
  • User Control: You control when and what data is backed up
  • Encrypted Backup: Cloud backups are encrypted before transmission
  • Your Google Account: Data is stored in your own Google Drive, not our servers

Third-Party Services

Google Play Services

  • In-App Purchases: We use Google Play Billing for Premium/Pro upgrades
  • Purchase Verification: Google verifies all purchase transactions
  • No Payment Data: We do not store credit card or payment information

Google Drive (Optional - Pro Users)

  • Backup Service: Pro users can optionally backup to their Google Drive
  • User Authorization: Requires explicit user permission and Google account linking
  • Limited Access: We only access files created by our app in your Drive
  • User Control: You can revoke access at any time through Google account settings

Your Data Rights

Access and Control

  • Full Access: View all your collection data within the app
  • Export Options: Export your collection data in multiple formats (JSON, Excel)
  • Data Portability: Take your data with you if you switch apps
  • Delete Data: Permanently delete your collection from the app

Children's Privacy

  • 13+ Only: This app is not intended for children under 13 years of age
  • No Knowingly Collection: We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13
  • Parental Notification: If we learn we have collected data from a child under 13, we will delete it immediately

Changes to This Policy

  • Notification: We will notify users of significant changes through app updates
  • Effective Date: Changes are effective immediately upon posting
  • Continued Use: Continued use of the app constitutes acceptance of changes
